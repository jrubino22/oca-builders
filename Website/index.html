A Remodeling Company That Cares

At OCA Builders we specialize in taking our clients vision and making it a reality. We provide everything you will need to build your dream home from A to Z. Starting with the our in-house architect, designer and engineer.  We will take the blue prints to be approved by the city and pull the permits so you don’t have to deal with those headaches. Our designer will be helping you put on the finishing touches, helping you choose paint, cabinets, counter tops or flooring. At OCA Builders, each one of our projects has a project manager. oUR Services

OCA Builders was established in 2009 in order to provide a broader range of remodeling services for homeowners in the following areas: Los Gatos, Saratoga, Campbell, San Jose, Cupertino, Santa Clara, Mountain View, Sunnyvale, Palo Alto, Menlo Park, Atherton, Redwood City, San Carlos, Belmont, Foster City, San Mateo, Bulingame. OCA Builders can start from doing work on your existing foundation of your home, something many new home contractors do not work on. In addition there is framing, installing the plumbing. Afterwards electrical work, then comes the Drywall to cover all services mentioned. To finish it off is flooring, tile, cabinets and painting services.
